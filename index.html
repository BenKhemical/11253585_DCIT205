Creative Activites

The Computer Science Department organize hackathons where students or professionals can collaborate intensively on projects within a specified time frame. This fosters creativity, problem-solving skills, and teamwork.

Sport Activites

Organize intramural sports leagues such as soccer, basketball, or volleyball within the computer science department. This provides an opportunity for students and faculty to engage in friendly competition and team building.

Acknowledging and rewarding hard work and dedication boosts morale, increases job satisfaction, and motivates individuals to continue putting in their best efforts..
